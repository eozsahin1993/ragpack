# PTO and Vacation Policy

**Solstice Labs — Employee Handbook**
Effective date: January 1, 2026

## Overview

Solstice Labs provides full-time employees with paid time off (PTO) to use for vacation, personal matters, or rest. We trust employees to manage their own time responsibly in coordination with their manager and team.

## Accrual

- Full-time employees accrue **18 PTO days per year**, credited at 1.5 days per month.
- Part-time employees (working 20–29 hours/week) accrue PTO on a pro-rated basis.
- New hires begin accruing PTO from their start date but may not use PTO during their first 30 days of employment.
- Unused PTO carries over into the next calendar year up to a maximum of **5 days**. Any balance above 5 days is forfeited on December 31st unless local law requires otherwise.

## Requesting Time Off

1. Submit a PTO request through the HR portal at least **5 business days** in advance for planned time off.
2. Your manager must approve the request before it is considered confirmed.
3. For time off longer than 5 consecutive business days, notify your manager at least **3 weeks** in advance so coverage can be arranged.
4. Sick time does not require advance notice — notify your manager as soon as reasonably possible.

## Sick Leave

Sick leave is tracked separately from PTO. Employees receive **10 paid sick days per year**, which do not roll over. A doctor's note is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## Holidays

Solstice Labs observes 11 paid company holidays per year, published annually on the HR portal in December for the following year. Employees in regions with different statutory holidays should consult their local HR contact for the applicable schedule.

## Unlimited PTO Exception

Employees at the Director level and above are on an unlimited PTO plan. There is no accrual or cap, but a minimum of 15 days off per year is expected, and all time off still requires manager approval.
