# Code of Conduct

**Solstice Labs — Employee Handbook**
Effective date: January 1, 2026

## Our Commitment

Solstice Labs is committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and safe workplace for every employee, contractor, and visitor. This Code of Conduct applies to all company spaces, whether physical, virtual, or company-sponsored events.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect, regardless of role, seniority, or background.
- Communicate directly and constructively; assume good intent, but address concerns rather than letting them go unspoken.
- Give credit where it's due, and be honest about mistakes.
- Protect confidential company, client, and colleague information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are grounds for disciplinary action, up to and including termination:

- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying based on race, gender, age, religion, disability, sexual orientation, or any other protected characteristic.
- Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith.
- Falsifying company records, expense reports, or time tracking.
- Sharing confidential company or client information outside authorized channels.
- Working under the influence of alcohol or drugs in a way that impairs job performance or safety.

## Reporting a Concern

Employees who witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct should report it through one of the following channels:

1. Your manager (if the concern does not involve them directly).
2. HR at `hr@solsticelabs.example` or the anonymous reporting form on the HR portal.
3. The Ethics Hotline at 1-800-555-0199, available 24/7 and operated by an independent third party.

All reports are investigated promptly and confidentially, to the extent possible. Retaliation against anyone making a good-faith report is strictly prohibited and is itself a violation of this policy.

## Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose any outside employment, financial interest, or personal relationship that could reasonably create a conflict of interest with their role at Solstice Labs. Disclosure forms are available on the HR portal and should be submitted before the conflict arises, where possible.

## Gifts and Entertainment

Employees may accept gifts from vendors or clients valued under **$100**. Anything above that must be disclosed to your manager and may need to be declined or donated, depending on the circumstances.

## Acknowledgment

All employees are required to review and acknowledge this Code of Conduct annually through the HR portal.
