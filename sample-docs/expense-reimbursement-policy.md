# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Solstice Labs — Finance Handbook**
Effective date: January 1, 2026

## General Principle

Employees may be reimbursed for reasonable, business-related expenses incurred while performing their job. When in doubt about whether an expense qualifies, ask your manager before spending.

## Submitting Expenses

- Submit expenses through the Expense Portal within **30 days** of the purchase date. Expenses submitted after 60 days will not be reimbursed except in extenuating circumstances approved by Finance.
- Attach an itemized receipt for any expense over **$25**. Credit card statements alone are not sufficient documentation.
- Expenses are reimbursed on the next standard payroll cycle after approval.

## Travel

| Category | Limit |
|---|---|
| Domestic flights | Economy class; upgrade allowed for flights over 6 hours |
| Hotel | Up to **$250/night** in standard cities, **$350/night** in high-cost cities (list maintained by Finance) |
| Meals while traveling | Up to **$75/day**, itemized receipts required over $25 |
| Ground transportation | Rideshare, taxi, or rental car; no reimbursement for personal vehicle mileage over 200 miles without pre-approval |

International travel follows the same limits unless otherwise negotiated with Finance in advance for the specific trip.

## Client and Team Meals

Client meals are reimbursable at reasonable cost with attendee names and business purpose noted in the expense report. Team meals (e.g. team lunches, offsite dinners) require manager pre-approval and are capped at **$40 per person**.

## Software and Subscriptions

Employees may expense work-related software and subscriptions up to **$100/month** without additional approval. Anything above that requires manager sign-off and should generally go through IT procurement instead of personal expense.

## Conference and Training

Solstice Labs covers conference registration, travel, and lodging for approved professional development, up to **$3,000 per employee per year**. Requests must be submitted at least **3 weeks** before the event via the Learning & Development form.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are never reimbursable: alcohol at solo meals, personal entertainment, traffic or parking fines, gym memberships (see Benefits Guide for wellness stipend instead), and first-class flight upgrades without prior approval.
