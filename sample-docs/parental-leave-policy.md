# Parental and Family Leave Policy

**Solstice Labs — Employee Handbook**
Effective date: January 1, 2026

## Overview

Solstice Labs provides paid leave for employees welcoming a new child, whether by birth, adoption, or foster placement, as well as leave for caring for family members with serious health conditions.

## Parental Leave

### Eligibility

All full-time employees are eligible for parental leave starting on their first day of employment. There is no minimum tenure requirement.

### Leave Duration

- **Birthing parent**: 16 weeks paid leave.
- **Non-birthing parent / partner**: 10 weeks paid leave.
- **Adoption or foster placement**: 10 weeks paid leave for the primary caregiver, 6 weeks for the secondary caregiver.

Leave must be taken within **12 months** of the birth, adoption, or placement, and may be split into two blocks with manager approval (for example, some weeks immediately after birth and additional weeks later in the year).

### Pay During Leave

Parental leave is paid at **100% of base salary**. Leave does not count against PTO accrual and does not affect the annual PTO balance.

## Family Care Leave

Employees may take up to **4 weeks of paid family care leave per year** to care for an immediate family member (spouse, child, or parent) with a serious health condition. A note from the family member's healthcare provider is required for leave exceeding 5 consecutive days.

## Returning to Work

### Phased Return

Employees returning from parental leave may request a phased return, working reduced hours at full pay for up to **2 weeks**, with manager approval.

### Benefits Continuation

Health insurance and other benefits continue uninterrupted during any paid leave under this policy. Employees on leave beyond 16 weeks should contact HR to discuss benefits continuation options.

## Requesting Leave

Notify your manager and HR as early as reasonably possible, ideally at least **30 days** before the expected leave start date for planned leave (such as an expected birth or adoption date). Submit the Leave Request form on the HR portal, which will guide you through the required documentation.

## Questions

Contact `hr@solsticelabs.example` or schedule time with your HR business partner.
