# Remote and Hybrid Work Policy

**Solstice Labs — Employee Handbook**
Effective date: January 1, 2026

## Work Modes

Solstice Labs supports three work modes, assigned per role and team based on business needs:

- **Remote** — employee works from a home office or approved remote location full-time.
- **Hybrid** — employee works from a Solstice Labs office at least **2 days per week**, remaining days remote.
- **Office-based** — employee works from a Solstice Labs office 5 days per week (required for roles involving lab equipment, hardware, or in-person client work).

Your work mode is set by your manager during onboarding and documented in the HR system. Changing work modes requires manager and HR approval.

## Home Office Stipend

Remote and hybrid employees receive a one-time **$750 home office stipend** during their first 60 days, plus an ongoing **$50/month** internet and utilities allowance. Stipend requests are submitted through the Expense Portal under the "Home Office" category.

## Core Hours

All employees, regardless of work mode, are expected to be reachable during **core hours**: 10:00 AM–3:00 PM in their team's primary time zone. Outside of core hours, employees may structure their schedule flexibly, subject to manager agreement.

## Working From Another Country

Employees may work from a different country for up to **30 consecutive days per year** without a formal transfer, provided:

1. The destination is not on the company's restricted-travel list (see IT & Security Policy).
2. Manager and HR are notified at least **2 weeks** in advance.
3. The employee remains reachable during their normal working hours in their home time zone.

Working abroad for longer than 30 days requires a formal international assignment agreement due to tax and legal implications.

## Equipment

Solstice Labs provides a company laptop to all employees. Remote employees may request a monitor, keyboard, and chair through the Equipment Portal, shipped to their home address at no cost. Equipment remains company property and must be returned within 14 days of departure.

## Office Access

Hybrid and office-based employees badge into the office using the Solstice Labs mobile app. Visitors must be registered at least 24 hours in advance through the front desk system.
